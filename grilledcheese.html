Grilled Cheese Recipe

Grilled Cheese Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white sliced bread
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
    • 1 slice of swiss cheese
  • 1 slice of gouda cheese
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • OPTIONAL: cooked bacon strips

Recipe Steps

  1. Place a pan on a medium/high heat stove
  2. Add all slices of cheese in between the slices of bread
  3. If using cooked bacon, place bacon in between the bread with cheese
  4. Lather both sides of the sandwich with butter
  5. Place sandwich in the pan
  6. When the one side of the sandwich is toasted, flip it and cook the other side
  7. When both sides of the sandwich have finished cooking, pull off of pan and serve
