Sandwich Recipe

Sandwich Ingredients

  • 2 slices of any sliced bread
  • 1 tbsp of mayo
  • 4 oz of any sliced deli meat
  • 2 slices of any cheese
  • OPTIONAL: lettuce slices, tomato slices, sliced onion

Recipe Steps

  1. If toasting the sandwich: Place both slices of bread in the toaster for approximately 2 minutes
  2. Once the bread is ready, spread one side of each slice with mayo
  3. Add your sliced deli meat on one side of the bread
  4. Add your cheese on top of your deli meat side
  5. OPTIONAL: if using additional sandiwch ingredients, add them on top of the cheese
  6. Once all sandwich ingredients are added, close the sandwich with the second slice of bread
