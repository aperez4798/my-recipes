Pizza Recipe

Pizza Ingredients

  • 4 oz of raw pizza dough
  • 1 small can of pizza marinara sauce
  • 10oz of freshly shredded mozzarella cheese
  • OPTIONAL: any topping you desire
  • pizza cutter

Recipe Steps

  1. Sprinkle a thin layer of flour onto a clean counter top surface
  2. Roll out the dough until you form a thin circular disc
  3. With a ladel or spoon, spread out some of the marinara sauce onto the raw dough
  4. Generously drizzle mozzarella cheese on top of the pizza. Make sure to get total coverage!
  5. If you are using any toppings, place the toppings on top of the people in this step
  6. Place the pizza in a 400 degree oven and bake for 20 minutes or until dough is golden brown
  7. When pizza is done cooking, pull out of over and cut into sections using a pizza cutter
